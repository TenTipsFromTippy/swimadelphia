Kelly

4231 Lansdowne Dr, Philadelphia, PA 19131

Kelly Pool (or, as some locals like to say, the “West Philly Pool”, is generally regarded as the the nicest, most beautiful pool in the city. Kelly is in historical Fairmount park, right next to the Please Touch Museum. It is a gorgeous place to swim laps, but the hours can vary a bit from day to day. Check their facebook page for any updates.
