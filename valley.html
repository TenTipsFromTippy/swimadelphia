Valley Beach

1160 1st Ave, King of Prussia, PA 19406

Open seasonally, this 21+ pool is a sandy hangout in the midst of a casino parking lot. But after enjoying a margarita or two, you’ll start to believe you’re somewhere near Tulum. Valley Beach boasts an open-air bar with poolside dining, private cabanas, a sandy beach, live entertainment and more. It’s available to overnight guests of the hotel or by purchasing a $10 day pass.
