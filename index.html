Hey, it’s hot out. Go for a swim.

Fortunately, Philadelphia has more public pools per capita than any other major US city. To use one, you don’t need to demonstrate proof of residency, you can simply show up and take a dip.

This map highlights some of the city’s best public pools. You’ll also find some private pools which allow folks to buy a day pass (the premium private clubs are restricted to members only and have years-long waiting lists) Pools listed here include the cost for a daypass, and who can buy one (i.e., some clubs are only for ages 21 and up)
